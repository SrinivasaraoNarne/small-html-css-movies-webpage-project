List of My Most Favorite movies

Avengers

"Avengers" is a blockbuster superhero film series by Marvel Studios, featuring iconic characters like Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor. The movies showcase epic battles and interconnected storylines.

Fast and Furious

"Fast & Furious" is a high-octane action film franchise known for its thrilling car races, heists, and a focus on family. It features a mix of adrenaline-pumping action and character-driven dynamics.

Pirates of the Caribbean

"Pirates of the Caribbean" is a swashbuckling adventure film series by Disney, starring Johnny Depp as the charismatic pirate Jack Sparrow. The franchise is known for its supernatural elements, humor, and maritime escapades.

Mission impossible

"Mission: Impossible" is a spy-action film series starring Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt. Known for its intense stunts and intricate plots, the franchise follows the missions of a skilled IMF agent and his team.

RRR

"RRR" is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language period action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. Set in the 1920s, it features fictionalized accounts of two Indian freedom fighters and their roles in fighting against the British Raj.

Bahubali

"Baahubali" is an Indian film franchise directed by S. S. Rajamouli. Known for its grandeur and visual effects, the series includes "Baahubali: The Beginning" and "Baahubali: The Conclusion." It follows the journey of a prince named Baahubali and his epic struggles for justice and power.
